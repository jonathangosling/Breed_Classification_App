Note: The confidence is the predicted probability of the estimation being correct, assuming that the breed is one of the 37 listed. As is becoming common knowledge with the mainstream adoption of ChatGPT, AI can be confidently wrong. This model is no different. The predictions can be incorrect and the model often gets confused between similar-looking breeds. In testing, the model predicted the correct breed 90% of the time.
